About Us

Satpuda Global School embodies the dynamic spirit of schooling.

Liberal education which also enjoys equitable educational opportunities is the day’s need. A formal schooling environment that provides for the global standards of infrastructure, aesthetics design, amenities etc. marks this unique Global School.

This is the first in the district and just the 10th in the whole state. Undoubtedly this is the first in any Indian town of this size and population; the intention is not to add another feather but consistently evolve organically. We believe Cambridge curriculum adds to the 21st Century requirements of compassionate humanity, critical and creative thinking tools in education, enterprise, communication etc.

We heartily welcome you to partake in this journey!
