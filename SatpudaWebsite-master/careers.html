Careers

Playing at Satpuda Global School is joyful and challenging in an environment of inclusion and freedom. Playing in Satpuda Global School means to be accommodative to the multiple versions of insightful learning!

Playing at Satpuda Global School means to know the rules of a truly working democracy; Being comfortable with the institutional means of implementing democratic principles; to have a belief that you can make a worthy contribution towards the consciousness of a young learner. Satpuda Global School offers numerous opportunities for excellence and growth.

Uniquely designed workshops, periodic retreats with the school family and training programmes under eminent educationists all offer extremely good learning environment in the school.

Qualities of a player:

A member of the Satpuda Global School family is expected to have a deep knowledge of one’s own self; of one’s own learning styles, sensations, teaching methodology, ideological inclinations and every other thing which pertains to the self.

A member of the Satpuda Global School family should have a deep knowledge of the child which comes from an unconditional love for the child and from an intense desire to contribute positively towards the consciousness of a child.

Should exhibit versatility in the knowledge of the external world: physical, chemical, Geographical, Historical etc. Deep understanding of the subject being taught and ability to debate with an open mind with the existing systems of beliefs and systems. An extraordinary ability to constantly destructure and deconstruct knowledge.

A member of Satpuda Global School family also has a deep sense of belongingness- a feeling of oneness and family within the school and also at large. Having a deep sense of one’s self-worth and does all that’s required to maintain one’s self-image!

National Curriculum:

A Post Graduate or a Graduate with a B.Ed is a mandatory requirement for working at Satpuda Global School.
