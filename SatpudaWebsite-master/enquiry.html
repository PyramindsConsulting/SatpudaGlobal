Eligibility Criteria

Admissions are open up to Grade VI for Cambridge Primary at Satpuda Global School, Betul.

Cambridge Primary, which is typically for learners aged 5 to 11 years, provides a world-class curriculum to develop learner skills and understanding in English, Mathematics and Science while ensuring that the child develops holistically.

Eligibility Criteria is as follows:
For Grade 1: Age 5-6 years old, completion of Kindergarten 1&2 from any curriculum
For Grade 2: Age 6-7 years old, completion of Class I from any curriculum
For Grade 3: Age 7-8 years old, completion of Class II from any curriculum
For Grade 4: Age 8-9 years old, completion of Class III from any curriculum
For Grade 5: Age 9-10 years old, completion of Class IV from any curriculum
For Grade 6: Age 10-11 years old, completion of Class V from any curriculum

Admission is facilitated after an interaction with both the child and parents.
