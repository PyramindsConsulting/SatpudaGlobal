Cambridge Programmes are for 5-19 year olds divided into 4 stages:

  • Cambridge Primary
  • Cambridge Secondary1
  • Cambridge Secondary 2 and
  • Cambridge Advanced.

Each stage builds on the learners' development in the previous stage.


At Cambridge Primary we use ‘active learning’ where our learners are active in the learning process by building knowledge and understanding in response to learning opportunities provided by their teacher. This means that learners take increasing responsibility for their learning, and that teachers are enablers and activators of learning.

Learning is predominantly student-centred, enquiry based, experiential, problem-based or discovery learning. Learners learn by addressing and posing scientific questions, analysing evidence, connecting such evidence to pre-existing theoretical knowledge, drawing conclusions, and reflecting upon their findings.
